Welcome to Byte me, where community meets sustainability. We’re on a mission to tackle hunger, reduce waste, and bring neighborhoods together. By connecting those in need with surplus food from local restaurants and grocery stores, we’re creating a more connected and sustainable world—one meal at a time.

Our Story

As students and low-income households face rising food costs, we recognized the huge amount of perfectly edible food being discarded daily by local restaurants and grocery stores. We wanted to change that. Our journey started with a vision to create a platform that links excess food with those who need it most—without waste. Today, Byte me connects communities and businesses through a unique system that redistributes surplus food in real time.

Our Offerings

At Byte me, we’re proud to offer a fast, easy, and sustainable solution to hunger and food waste. Through our online platform, users can access real-time listings of surplus food from local restaurants, cafes, and grocery stores. Whether you’re a student looking for a quick meal or a family on a tight budget, our platform makes it easy to find food that’s close by and ready to be enjoyed.

Our Promise

We promise to be more than just a food-sharing platform. We’re committed to creating a sustainable, accessible solution to hunger while reducing food waste.
